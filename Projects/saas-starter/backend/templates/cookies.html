Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Authentication Cookies: We use cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. We use session cookies to maintain your login state.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. We use persistent cookies to remember your preferences.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device:

  • Stripe: For payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Analytics Services: To understand website usage (if enabled)

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies
  • Browser settings: You can also set your browser to notify you when cookies are being used
  • Deleting cookies: You can delete cookies that are already on your device

Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about cookie usage
  • Give or withdraw consent for non-essential cookies
  • Access information about cookies used

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.
